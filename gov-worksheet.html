Section F — Vendor Audit Checklist
Renewal Pricing: Year 2+ pricing disclosed and documented
Escalation Cap: Annual price increase cap specified (recommend ≤5%)
Data Portability: Exit terms and data export provisions confirmed
Usage Audit Rights: Agency can audit actual vs. billed usage
Model Disclosure: AI model(s) powering service identified
Security Compliance: FedRAMP / StateRAMP / applicable security certification verified
SLA Terms: Uptime guarantee and remedies documented
