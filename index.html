Methodology

Compute CPI tracks the changing cost of a representative "basket" of AI work, expressed as an index where February 2026 = 100.

The basket includes six workload categories: chat/drafting, summarization, classification, coding, judgment/reasoning, and long-context synthesis. Each category is weighted to reflect typical institutional usage patterns.
