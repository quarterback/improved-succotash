Occupant

Daily indices for the AI compute market.

Free. Open methodology. By Ron Bronson.

What it publishes.

Three indices, updated daily. Compute CPI tracks the price of inference across model tiers. AEAI tracks activity — tokens, spend, energy. LDI tracks the cost margin between machine work and human work.

Each one is framed as a question, not a product. See the inquiries →

How the numbers are made.

Vendor-independent. No referral fees, no paid placement, no consulting attached. Index values publish as JSON. Price discrepancies above ten percent trigger a manual review before they reach the page.

Nothing ships that cannot be defended in writing.
