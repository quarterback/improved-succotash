Note on "per million tokens": One million tokens ≈ 750,000 words. A typical government document query uses 2,000-5,000 tokens. At $5/MTok, that's about $0.01-0.025 per query.

Questions to Ask Vendors

  • What's your per-token or per-query pricing? (Not just per-seat)
  • What model(s) power the service? What's the Arena ELO?
  • Is pricing locked for the contract term, or variable?
  • What happens to pricing after Year 1?
  • Do you charge for failed queries or retries?
  • What's included in the "seat" vs. metered usage?
  • How do you handle data residency and FedRAMP?
  • Can we audit actual usage vs. billed usage?

Red Flags

  • Won't disclose which AI models power the service
  • Pricing significantly above market benchmarks
  • "Unlimited" plans with hidden fair-use clauses
  • Year 2+ pricing not disclosed upfront
  • No usage dashboard or audit capability
  • Requires multi-year without price protection
  • Charges for "AI processing" as separate line item
  • Can't explain per-token vs per-seat math
