About Occupant

The standard for AI compute value.

What We Do

We aggregate AI pricing and activity data into benchmarks you can track over time. Public data infrastructure for procurement, budgeting, and strategic planning.

Compute CPI measures price deflation across AI model tiers—tracking how fast compute is getting cheaper. AI Activity Index (AEAI) measures economic volume—tracking token throughput, spend, and energy consumption. Together, they give you the complete picture: where prices are going and how fast the market is growing.

Updated daily from 2,300+ models.

Methodology

Compute CPI aggregates pricing data into a volume-weighted basket across four workload tiers. AEAI tracks economic activity through token volumes (60%), inferred spend (30%), and energy consumption (10%). Cross-validated across sources. Discrepancies flagged before publication. January 2025 = 100 for both indices.

Index Composition Weighting
$CPI Composite of all tiers Volume-weighted
$AIU Token volumes, spend, energy 60/30/10 weighted basket

Build Cost Indices ($START, $AGENT, $THRU) apply fixed workload mixes to current tier prices. Track costs relevant to your specific use patterns.
