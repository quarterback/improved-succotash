About Occupant

Trust + Decision Engineering. We build instruments that reprice what institutions think is real.

What We Do

Occupant works at the intersection of AI systems and institutional decision-making. As organizations increasingly depend on AI for judgment—not just automation—they need new instruments for understanding cost, quality, and trust.

Public Benchmarks

The Compute CPI provides a standardized reference for AI pricing. Organizations use it to budget, benchmark vendor proposals, and track how their costs change over time.

  • Daily index updates
  • Multi-source validation
  • Government-specific benchmarks

Advisory

We work directly with procurement, platform, and leadership teams. Our focus: building decision architectures that remain auditable as AI capabilities expand.

  • Decision budget frameworks
  • Escalation path design
  • Governance architecture

Independence

The Occupant Index operates independently of model providers, cloud platforms, and API resellers. Index methodology changes are documented and disclosed prior to implementation.

We don't accept sponsored placements, referral arrangements, or payments that could influence rankings or calculations. Our revenue comes from advisory work and institutional data access—not from the companies whose prices we track.
